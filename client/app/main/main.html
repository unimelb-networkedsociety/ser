STEM Education Resource

The STEM Education Resource (SER) is a free online resource to support the teaching and learning of STEM at Years 9 & 10.

SER allows students to complete four modules to solve an open-ended problem. The modules support students’ abilities to undertake STEM investigations by developing the high-level knowledge and skills required to solve real-life, interdisciplinary problems.

SER is scheduled for launch mid-2018. Register below for updates.
